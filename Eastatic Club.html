Music For Good

The Renuka Malkanthi Alwis “Music for Good” Academy at our flagship venue in Seenigama was set- up thank to Dr. Lankanatha Alwis from the UK, in loving memory of her mother. Our aspiring musicians from rural Sri Lanka enjoy musical training and understanding during this course. Many donations of musical equipment has helped the Academy to grow!
